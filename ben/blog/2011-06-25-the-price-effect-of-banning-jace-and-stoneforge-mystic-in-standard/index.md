---
title: The price effect of banning Jace and Stoneforge Mystic in Standard
---

A few days ago,
[Jace, the Mind Sculptor](http://mtg.throwingbones.com/card/33477) and
[Stoneforge Mystic](http://mtg.throwingbones.com/card/33287) were
banned by Wizards. What is unexpected is that the price for a [Foil Jace](http://mtg.throwingbones.com/card/33372) is still high, close to $200.  This may be because the are not many for sale, so their value remains high, or it may be a delayed effect of the banning, we will have to watch and see.

Jace is used in other formats, so his price may stay stable, but you
can see both the
[Stoneforge Mystic](http://mtg.throwingbones.com/card/33287) and
[Foil Stoneforge Mystic](http://mtg.throwingbones.com/card/33432) are down since June 19th (46% and 30%, respectively)
